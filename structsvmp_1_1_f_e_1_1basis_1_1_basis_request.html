Detailed Description

Runtime description of a basis to construct.

A request identifies exactly one construction target – a named ElementType layout, or a reference BasisTopology with an explicit order – plus the family and field policy; basis_factory::create() validates and builds from it. The spline/NURBS fields are reserved for future families and are unused by the scalar Lagrange/Serendipity factory.
